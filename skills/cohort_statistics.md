# Cohort Statistics & Genomic Classifications

This skill folder controls coding standards, peer study correlations, and meta-analyses.

## Clinical Research Directives
- **Standard Coding Vocabulary**: Map extracted physical parameters and pharmaceuticals to standard identifiers (LOINC for lab tests, SNOMED-CT for diagnoses, and RxNorm for medications).
- **Phenotypic Registry Matching**: Correlate patient biobank parameters or laboratory indicators with larger national cohorts (such as the UK Biobank, Framingham Heart Study, or NHANES).
- **Study Significance Metrics**: Reference confidence intervals, p-values, odds ratios (OR), or hazard ratios (HR) from relevant peer-reviewed clinical research or randomized control trials.
- **Data Integration Grid**: Render structural information using common data models suitable for OMOP or CDISC CDM ingestion pipelines.
