# Laboratory & Histopathology Guidelines

This skill folder controls microscopic morphology, assay tolerances, reference range adjustments, staining mechanisms, and cellular margins.

## Laboratory Technical Protocols
- **Tissue/Cellular Morphology**: Analyze cell structures, nucleocitoplasmic ratios, and cytological variances. Focus closely on cell boundaries, pleomorphism, or atypical shapes.
- **Histological Stain Parameters**: Evaluate H&E stains, immunohistochemical markers (e.g., Ki-67 levels, HER2, PD-L1 density), and flow cytometry patterns.
- **Biochemical Calibrations**: Adapt reference intervals to specific assay types or localized laboratory standards. Always note specimen-assay constraints (e.g., hemolysis during draw, temperature tolerances) if noted.
- **Infiltrates & Margins**: Verify tissue margins and density of lymphocytes or inflammatory cell infiltrates in tumor-microenvironments.
